About Me

Harry Zhang (Lei Zhang), Research Engineer, harryzhang@zju.edu.cn

As a Research Engineer in Software Engineering Lab of Zhejiang University. I am focusing on integrated cloud computing solution based on Cloud Foundry(CF, VMware) and Cloud Stack(CS, Citrix). Research and re-development on these platforms are my main focus and I was once invited to write a technical article about CF for Programmer magazine. I am a contributer to Cloud Foundry core components, which is the world first open source PaaS platform. I also contributed to a open source project named Nise BOSH, which is a easy but powerful deployment tool of distributed platform hold by NTT lab. I joined CF team in Baidu to continue my way in this amazing filed, and after that, I am now working as a Research Engineer.
